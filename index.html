Biography

I’m an Assistant Professor at EURECOM with the S3 group. I’m interested in various aspects of cyber-physical and wireless systems security.

I hold a PhD in Computer Science from the Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD). During my PhD I’ve researched ways to design, implement and evaluate secure cyber-physical and wireless systems. As part of my PhD I spent six months at the Computer Science Department of the University of Oxford as a visiting researcher advised by Kasper Rasmussen, and ten months as a visiting researcher at the Helmholtz Center for Information Security (CISPA) advised by Nils Ole Tippenhauer.
